Macchiato

In Italian, the term ‘macchiato’ translates as ‘marked’ or ‘stained’, meaning a stained or marked coffee. The macchiato is an espresso coffee drink, topped with a small amount of foamed or steamed milk to allow the taste of the espresso to still shine through.

Ingredients

  1. Fresh grounded coffee beans
  2. Cold milk

You also need

  1. small cup, 160-175ml capacity

Directions

  1. Brew one shot of fresh espresso with espresso machine.
  2. Steam milk to 160°, and hold the steam wand just below the surface to create foam.
  3. Pour milk into espresso to taste and spoon foam on top.
